Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie
Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cramberries, and creamy brie. It will become your favourite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the Focaccia

  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the Topping

  • 4 cups (500g) all purpose flour
